# Emotional Pacing

## Emotion Escalation Formula

### Universal Rhythm
```
Setup (70%) → Climax (10%) → Echo (20%)
```

### Tear-jerker Three Steps
1. **Connect**: Make the reader love the character
2. **Hope**: The moment right before the character achieves their goal
3. **Shatter**: Use the character's crucial choice to break everything

### Hype Building
1. **Suppression**: Reader and MC suffer together
2. **Breaking Point**: An event that can't be endured
3. **Release**: Emotion and power released simultaneously
4. **Echo**: Bystander reactions (awe/admission/respect)

## Atmosphere Through Environment

| Scene | Keywords |
|-------|---------|
| Sadness | Overcast, rain, dark tones, cold wind, fallen leaves |
| Tension | Noise, racing heartbeat, lengthening shadows |
| Warmth | Warm light, small space, food aroma, slow movement |
| Mystery | Fog, echoes, light halos, hollow footsteps |
| Oppression | Door cracks, whispers, dripping, silhouettes |

## Show Don't Tell Emotion

- Don't say "touched": write the throat moving, the eyes stinging
- Don't say "angry": write knuckles whitening, breathing deepening
- Don't say "sad": write silence, spacing out, tears falling into the bowl
- Don't say "love": write remembered details, subconscious protection
